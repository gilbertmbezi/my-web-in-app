# My Web In App – Privacy Policy (Web Viewer Feature)

*Last updated: March 2026*

## 1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how **My Web In App** ("we", "our", or "the app") handles user information when using the feature that allows users to enter a URL and view a website within the app.

By using this feature, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

---

## 2. Information We Collect

We aim to collect **minimal to no personal data**.

### a. User-Entered URLs

* The app may temporarily process URLs entered by users
* URLs are not stored permanently unless explicitly saved by the user (e.g., in notes)

### b. Automatic Data (via Web Content)

When you load a website inside the app, the website itself may collect data such as:

* IP address
* Device information
* Cookies and tracking data

This data is collected **by the website**, not by My Web In App.

---

## 3. How We Use Information

We use information only to:

* Load and display the requested web page
* Improve app functionality and performance

We do **not** sell, rent, or share user data with third parties.

---

## 4. Third-Party Websites

The in-app browser allows access to external websites. Please note:

* Each website has its own privacy policy
* We are **not responsible** for the content or privacy practices of third-party sites
* Users should review the privacy policies of any site they visit

---

## 5. Cookies and Tracking

My Web In App itself does not use cookies for tracking.

However, websites opened within the app may:

* Use cookies
* Track user behavior
* Store session data

These are controlled by the respective websites.

---

## 6. Data Storage

* My Web In App does not store browsing history by default
* Any saved data (such as notes containing URLs) is stored locally on the user’s device

Users are responsible for managing and deleting their own data.

---

## 7. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect the app environment. However:

* We cannot guarantee the security of third-party websites
* Users should avoid entering sensitive information on untrusted sites

---

## 8. Children’s Privacy

My Web In App is not specifically directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

---

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted with a revised "Last updated" date.
